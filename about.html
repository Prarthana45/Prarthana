Our Story

A Legacy of
Engineering
Excellence

Prarthana Industries was founded in 1996 with a clear vision: to manufacture and export precision transmission products and machinery parts that industries across India and abroad could rely on without compromise.

Starting from a focused workshop in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad, we grew steadily by earning the trust of engineers, procurement managers, and OEM companies who demand quality, consistency, and reliability in every component.

Today, we serve customers across India and export to international markets — delivering the same precision and commitment to quality that has defined us since day one.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped Us

1976
Foundation
Mr. Chandravadan Pancholi started the business of trading in machinery parts and transmission products, laying the foundation of what would become Prarthana Industries.
1996
Company Established
Prarthana Industries formally established in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad. Expanded from trading into manufacturing of gear racks, rack & pinion, and precision transmission components for the Indian industrial market.
2020
Export Markets
First export orders fulfilled. Began serving clients in South-East Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond — delivering quality components across India and abroad with compliant packaging and full documentation.
2026
Today & Beyond
Serving 500+ clients across India and abroad with a full range of transmission products and machinery parts. Continuously growing — in capability, capacity, and geographic reach.
